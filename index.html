About Brainpup

Who we are

BrainPup is the world’s first engineering project showcase and open collaboration platform for learning and sharing knowledge.

We need you to help us make the world a smarter, more connected, better place. Remember, you don't have to be an engineer to be part of the engineering community!

Help us bridge the gap between students, professors, and industry, no matter where you live!

Students and the engineering community around the globe can learn about classes, professors, companies, concepts and everything in between. Professors and industry recruiters can find the most qualified candidates to join their lab or organization.
